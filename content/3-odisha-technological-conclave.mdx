## A Proud Achievement at Odisha Technological Conclave 2024

I had the privilege of participating in the **Odisha Technological Conclave-2024**, organized by **Ever Green Forum**, where our team secured **2nd position** in the **Model Exhibition**. This event, held at **Jayadev Bhawan, Bhubaneswar**, was a fantastic platform to showcase innovative ideas and collaborate with talented minds.

A heartfelt thank you to my mentors, institution **DRIEMS Polytechnic**, and my dedicated team for their invaluable support. Looking forward to many more opportunities to learn and grow!
